Bio

I am a second-year Ph.D. candidate (2021~) at Westlake University and Zhejiang University, advised by Stan Z. Li. I am aslo a member of Westlake drug discovery group. I strive to develop deep learning algorithms to benefit drug discovery, and I am also interested in weakly-supervised learning and unsupervised learning.

Looking forward to academic communications and feel free to contact me!
